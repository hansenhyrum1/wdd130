Content

Home page

This home page will hold basic information regarding soil and ground covering and when to use each. It will also include a tool for calculating the amount of material that one may need. It will show some tips and tricks and even some tools that you can use or rent in order to make things go faster.

Welcome to the Bulk Yard Bonanza! We are the specialists in helping you determine the right type of bulk materials that you need for your landscape or yard. We provide information and examples regarding soils, rocks, and bark.

Images for the Home page

Ground Cover

This page will hold all the materials including rocks and bark that are not soils, but a ground cover type of bulk material. It will list the different types and maintenance and care of them. It will also show that you can add a weed mat ground covering to the area in order to prevent weeds form coming up through your ground cover.

Ground Cover Images

Soil

This page will not only talk about all the different soils, it will dicuss the types of soil compositions and how to know what is best to use in every situation. It will talk about the micronutrients and organisms within the soil. Types of soil compositions and aspects of the soil will be covered in this page.
