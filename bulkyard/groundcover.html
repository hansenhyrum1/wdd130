Barks

Barks are a popular top dressing to garden and flower beds and options include nugget, shredded, and fine barks. These can be trucked in and distributed using wheelbarrows and buckets or a new technology called bark-blowing may be used. Trucks that work alike reverse vaccum cleaners use large tubes to evenly distribute bark across large areas.

Ground Cover

All ground cover, Bark and Rocks, can help to prevent weeds by blocking the growth of weeds up through the soil. Neither can do it perfectly, so it is always recommened to use weed control fabric underneath these ground covers, or else use some sort of vegetation control. Finding the colors of ground cover are based entirely on your preferences as only artifical materials will change color or fade over time.

Rocks

Rocks are a great decorative material to place in flower beds, walkways, drainage, patios, and more. There are colored riverstones, boulders, gravel, and sands. These are normally distributed with heavy equipment and tractors, but in some cases, such as a driveway, a dump truck and leave an evenly distributed path of rock.
