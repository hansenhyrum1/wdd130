Permeability

Permeability means the amount of water and air that can pass through the soils. A good soil has a good permeability that retains moisture, but also lets it pass through to the underlying sub-soils. Sands help water to pass through the soil and clays help to retain that moisture.

Nutrients

The different types of nutrients in the soil can be attributed to the organic materials found within it. Manure, compost, and aged bark all help to add different types of nutrients and organic material to the soil.

Soil PH

The PH of a soil measures the level of acidity or base in the soil. It is important to see what PH level works best for your plants, as it can be a huge determiner on the amount of fruit and color plants.
